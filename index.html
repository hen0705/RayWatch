Track cownose ray migration

Real-time sightings across the Chesapeake and Mid-Atlantic

Status

Cownose rays (Rhinoptera bonasus) are classified as Near Threatened due to bycatch and unregulated recreational fishing

Migration

They migrate seasonally in large groups along the Atlantic coast, moving north in spring and south in fall

Why it matters

Tracking sightings can inform migration patterns and support conservation efforts
